Return Policy

  1. Initiate a return for a delivered item within 3 days after its delivery by clicking here and go to orders, then click on the item(s) you wish to return.
  2. Select the number of items you wish to return, the reason for return, and provide more details to help us identify the issue.
  3. Let us handle it by picking up the item from your desired location.
  4. The mode of payment used by the client will be the same mode of payment used during reimbursement.
  5. Recheck the details of your return request, then submit it.
  6. Repack the item in its original packaging, including any of its accessories.
  7. Once your package is rerouted, we will do a final check, after which your refund will be made.
NOTE:
  • The return request is only valid if performed within the first 3 days from reception of the item.
  • Pickup fees will be deducted from the customer's payment for the specific item.
