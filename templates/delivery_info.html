Delivery Information

Our current delivery mode is door delivery and global packaging, with pickup stations being arrayed next in line to serve you better.

  • 1-2 working days for orders in and around Kampala CBD. (If your order is placed early, you could have it delivered earlier).
  • 2-3 working days for orders further away from the Kampala CBD neighborhood.
  • For orders across the Ugandan border, the exact delivery days will be provided according to the cargo plane instructions or DHL.

Payment Methods

  • For clients within the CBD, payment will either be cash on delivery or prepayment through provided mediums of payment.
  • For clients outside the CBD, payment will be prepayment through the provided mediums of payment.

Delivery Time

Delivery time is 7am to 5:30pm.

  • Delivery time starts from the day you place the order to the day one of our associates makes the first attempt to deliver the order.
  • Delivery will be attempted two times over 2 days, after which the item will be canceled if you are unreachable or unable to receive it.
NOTE:
  • If the item is canceled due to failed delivery attempts, clients who have made prepayment will be reimbursed with clear deductions of transportation fees.
