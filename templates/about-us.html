HELLO, WE'RE ARCRID

Arcrid is the home of African art, craft, and interior design - the place to buy handmade gifts and original artwork, sold by the artists and designers who have created them and their affiliated shops. Made with love, every purchase from Arcrid supports a genuine craftsperson.

WHAT WE DO

  • Empower Artisans: Arcrid provides a platform for artists and designers to sell their work without restrictions. Everyone is welcome as long as their work is original and handmade.
  • Serving Customers: We enable global access to art, craft, and interior design products through convenient and affordable online services.

WHY WE DO ARCRID?

  • Art & Craft Skills: We appreciate the creativity and talent behind handmade work.
  • Strength in Numbers: Artists can reach wider audiences without costly galleries.
  • Cottage Industries: Small-scale production supports creativity and local economies.
  • Meritocracy: Any skilled artisan can showcase their work.
  • Connectivity: Art enhances moods and environments, making spaces feel welcoming.

ABOUT OUR FOUNDER

Arcrid was founded in 2025 by Lurvin Elisha Atwiine, inspired by a love of art, craft, and interior design. Seeing the creativity of African craft communities and the potential of online shopping, Lurvin built Arcrid as a place where artists can showcase and sell their work.
